Jaipiur

Jaipur, the Pink City, is known for its royal heritage and stunning architecture. It’s a must-visit for history lovers.

Explore the majestic Amber Fort, City Palace, and the intricate Hawa Mahal.

Jaipur’s bazaars are full of colorful textiles, jewellery, and handicrafts that reflect Rajasthani culture.
